🫒 Fats: The Heart-Healthy Way

This plan uses olive oil as the primary fat. Always use extra-virgin cold-pressed. Avoid butter, coconut oil, and vegetable shortening. Avocados and nuts provide beneficial monounsaturated fats.

🧂 Sodium Rules

  • Target: under 1,500 mg/day for cardiac patients
  • Choose no-salt-added canned tomatoes and beans
  • Rinse all canned beans before using
  • Use low-sodium vegetable broth only
  • Skip added salt — use lemon, herbs, and garlic instead
  • Check tuna: choose "no salt added" or rinse well

🐟 Omega-3 Priority Foods

This plan prioritizes salmon, shrimp, tuna, and cod — all rich in omega-3 fatty acids that reduce inflammation and support heart rhythm. Aim for at least 2 servings of fatty fish per week (Days 1 and 7 cover this well).

🍽️ Portion Awareness

  • Fish/chicken: 4–5 oz per serving
  • Grains (cooked): ½ cup per serving
  • Olive oil: 1 tbsp max per meal
  • Nuts: 1 oz (small handful) per serving
  • Feta: 1–2 tbsp — it's salty, use sparingly

💊 Medication Notes

If taking warfarin (blood thinners), maintain consistent leafy green (spinach, arugula) intake week to week rather than varying dramatically. Grapefruit can interact with some cardiac medications — check with your doctor. This plan does not include grapefruit.

🥗 Meal Prep Tips

  • Cook a big batch of quinoa and farro on Day 1 — use all week
  • Pre-wash and dry all greens on shopping day
  • Hard-boil 4–6 eggs at once for quick access
  • Make lentil soup in double batch (Days 2 & 7 lentils)
  • Grill extra chicken on Day 1 for Day 4 Greek salad
